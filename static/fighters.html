Meet our Trainers

Jonpop Kiatphontip

Jompop has an extremely successful career fighting at the highest level in Thailand, formerly ranked No 1 in Rajadamnern stadium, he fought in the main event many times under the number one promotions team Onesongchai Promotions of the legendary promoter Songchai Ratanasuban .

Jompop has been fighting like most thai fighters since the age of 9yrs old from his home in Phattalung province of Thailand. Jompop fought extensively throughout the south of Thailand where he developed his exceptional reputation. By doing this he attracted the eye of Songchai's talent scouts. Jompop then went on to make his career in Bangkok where he firstly fought in the famous and world renound Lumpini Stadium, then shortly into his career Songchai moved his promotions across to Rajadamnern stadium. Jompop quickly made a extremely good name for himself through his own skills. Jompop fought exclusively under the Songchai promotions. He has shared the ring with some of Thailand’s biggest names such as Saenchainoi Toyotarayong, Chatchainoi Sitbenjamas, Puja Sor Suwanee, Dennarklang Sor Wirapan, Pongsing Kiatchansing and many more. Jompop retired from fighting at the age of 23 to further his education by completing his Degree in Law. After which he returned to Thailand and commenced the development of Kiatphontip Gym in Bangkok.

Teaching Skills and Experience Jompop has been training in Muay Thai for over 19 years. Coached and influenced a number of young talented fighters including Samsamut Kiatchongkao through two successful title challenges for the Rajadamnern and Thailand Lightweight titles.

Fighting Style Very technical fighter renound for his fast kicks, dangerous elbows and one of the best clinch and take down specialists there is.

Achievements • South Thailand Champion • Ranked No.1 in Rajadamnern Stadium

Fight Record Professional: 161 Fights Professional: 131 Wins

Maria Chu

Maria commenced training when she was aged 7 aswell as having her first fight at only 7yrs old also. After taking some time out Maria then spent 1 and a half years travelling around Thailand and training at a number of different camps. She has learnt many specific skills which have contributed to her success. Her main strengths as a fighter which she gained whilst in Thailand are her powerful punches, devastating head kicks as seen from her KO’s aswell as been very strong in clinch.

Teaching Skills and Experience Experienced and accomplished Muay Thai Trainer. Experienced in Coaching Boxercise, Circuit Classes.

Achievements • Whilst in Thailand Maria also fought in a number of Stadiums including bangla stadium Phuket, phattalung, baan klang stadium bangkok, nontabury stadium, nakornpatom stadium bangkok and Sangtiennoi Sor Rungrot's show on loy krathong festival 2009.

Simon Chu

Simon is a highly skilled Muay Thai and K1 specialist with over 20 years professional training, fighting and teaching under his belt. Sharing the ring and gym with the very best trainers and fighters in the world. He had his first Muay Thai fight at only 8 years old.

• Currently ranked No.1 Middleweight in the UK.

• 2 x European Champion.

• 4 x British Champion.

• Has fought and beat champions from all over the globe.

• He has been the main event on many super shows aired on such channels as Sky Sports, Pay Per View and ESPN.

• He has competed in such places as the Hard Rock Hotel/Casino - Las Vegas, Fight Code Tournament- Budapest, Rajadamnern Stadium - Bangkok, Buffallo Bills Casino - Las Vegas, S1 Tournament - Italy, M.E.N Arena - Manchester, Doncaster Dome and many others.

Fighting Style Simon is a strong technical fighter with fast boxing combinations, powerful kicks, jumping knees and devastating elbows. As Simon's nick name "Dynamite Fists" suggests he is always hunting for the KO, having the ability to achieve it with one shot.

Fight Record Amateur: 14 fights 13 wins Professional 42 fights 36 wins 22 KO's
