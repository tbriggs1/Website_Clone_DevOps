About Muay Thai

Muay Thai / Thai kick boxing is the national sport of Thailand. It is also known as “the Art of Eight Limbs”strong> due to the following body part being used fists, elbows, knees, legs (kicks). Muay Thai training develops cardio-vascular endurance, devasting power, speed and fighting spirit.
