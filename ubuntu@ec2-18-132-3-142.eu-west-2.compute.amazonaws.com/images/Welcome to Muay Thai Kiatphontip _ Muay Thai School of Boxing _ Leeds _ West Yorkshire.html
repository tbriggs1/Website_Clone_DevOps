About Muay Thai Boxing

Muay Thai / Thai kick boxing is the national sport of Thailand. It is also known as the Art of Eight Limbs due to the following body part being used fists, elbows, knees, legs (kicks). Muay Thai training develops cardio-vascular endurance, devasting power, speed and fighting spirit.

All our training sessions and classes are based in Leeds we do however offer 121 tuition starting at £30.00 per hour. Deals are available for block bookings.

Whether your a beginner or have martial arts background we are here to extend your knowledge of the traditional Muay Thai style of Kickboxing, taking you from novice to competition level if you require. If you are not quite sure if it's for you just give us a call and we will be happy to give you some guidance and book you in for an initial training session.
